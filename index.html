What is a Robot?

A robot is a machine that can perform tasks automatically or with human control. Robots are used in factories, hospitals, homes, and even in space.

Main Parts of a Robot

  • Controller (Brain of robot ‚Äì Arduino)
  • Sensors (Eyes & ears of robot)
  • Motors (Movement)
  • Power Supply (Battery)
  • Body / Chassis

How is a Robot Made?

  1. Decide the purpose of the robot
  2. Choose required sensors and motors
  3. Connect everything to the controller
  4. Write the program (code)
  5. Test and improve the robot

Types of Robots

  • Line Follower Robot
  • Obstacle Avoiding Robot
  • Industrial Robot
  • Medical Robot
  • Space Robot
